The Problem

A world built for
those who can hear

🗣️

Communication Barrier

Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face daily challenges in hospitals, schools, workplaces, and public spaces where interpreters are unavailable or unaffordable.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Medical emergencies become life-threatening when patients cannot communicate symptoms. Studies show deaf patients receive significantly worse care due to language barriers.

🎓

Educational Inequality

Many deaf children are placed in mainstream classrooms without proper support, falling behind peers due to lack of accessible communication tools and trained educators.
